Case Studies

Explore the success stories of our customers and how our solutions helped them overcome unique challenges.

Revin Sight and Revin Log Optimise Irrigation and Boost Efficiency.

Problem Statement

A large-scale farm was facing challenges in managing water resources efficiently, leading to uneven crop growth and high water usage.

Challenge

Traditional irrigation practices resulted in inconsistent soil moisture levels, water wastage, and reduced crop yields. The absence of real-time data made it difficult to optimize irrigation schedules, leading to inefficiencies.

Our Solution

By integrating Revin Sight’s real-time soil moisture monitoring with Revin Log’s farm management platform, the farm optimised its irrigation system. The solution provided actionable insights into soil conditions and weather patterns, enabling precise irrigation. This approach led to significant water savings, improved crop uniformity, and a noticeable increase in overall yield.

Revin Sight and Revin Skypulse Accelerate Climate-Resilient Crop Breeding.

Problem Statement

A research institution was struggling to develop climate-resilient crop varieties due to unpredictable weather patterns and insufficient environmental data.

Challenge

Collecting accurate data on soil conditions, temperature variations, and plant growth under stress was difficult, leading to delays in the breeding program. This lack of precise data extended the breeding cycle, delaying the release of new resilient crop varieties.

Our Solution

Revin Sight provided the institution with precise monitoring of soil health, temperature, and humidity, while Revin Skypulse delivered high-resolution imagery for detailed plant growth analysis. These tools enabled researchers to identify key traits for climate resilience and accelerate the breeding process, resulting in the timely release of new crop varieties with enhanced resilience.

Revin Log Enhances Traceability and Compliance in the Crop Value Chain.

Problem Statement

A major agricultural exporter struggled with ensuring traceability across its supply chain, leading to compliance issues and delays in approvals.

Challenge

Tracking the origin, quality, and movement of crops from farms to processing units was challenging, leading to delayed approvals and potential losses due to non-compliance with international standards.

Our Solution

Revin Log was implemented as a comprehensive farm management platform that enabled end-to-end traceability from fields to processing units. The platform’s AI-powered quality analysis ensured consistent product quality, while its traceability features met international standards. This solution improved compliance, reduced shipment rejections, and ensured smooth operations across the supply chain.

Revin Skypulse and Revin Sight Revolutionise Crop Monitoring and Harvest Planning.

Problem Statement

A cooperative of farmers faced challenges in monitoring crop health across large fields, leading to suboptimal harvest timing and reduced profits.

Challenge

Manual observations were used to determine harvest timing, resulting in inconsistent crop quality and missed opportunities for maximising yield.

Our Solution

Revin Skypulse provided high-resolution drone imagery for continuous crop monitoring, while Revin Sight offered real-time data on soil moisture and plant health. These combined insights allowed the farmers to optimise their harvest timing based on accurate, up-to-date information. The implementation of these solutions led to improved crop quality, better yield, and increased revenue for the cooperative.
