About Us

Revin Krishi is an Agtech startup democratising Data Driven Agriculture using IoT and Big Data. We design integrated full stack solutions for alternative farming, offering sensing, automation, and traceability solutions for smart farming, ensuring reliable and sustainable food at scale.


Emerging from IIT Palakkad, Revin Krishis mission revolves around building engineering solutions that enhance human lives by cultivating the right crops with best practices at the appropriate locations using relevant technologies at the right scale to feed the world sustainably.

Products at a Glance

Our flagship product, Revin Sight, is an intelligent in-field monitoring device that provides real-time insights into your crops. By combining weather, plant, and soil data with advanced analytics, Revin Sight offers actionable recommendations to boost productivity and profitability. This robust device helps you make informed decisions, optimise resource utilisation, and reduce risks.

Read More

Revin Spectra is a drone service with digitising and automating agriculture with high-resolution imagery for actionable and accurate insights. With features like crop monitoring, soil assessment, fertility assessment, yield assessment, crop protection, and harvest planning. This robust service helps you make faster, data-driven decision-making to optimise yield.

Read More

Revin Log is a comprehensive farm management platform designed to streamline operations and improve efficiency. With features like climate smart advisory, AI-powered quality analysis, and pest/disease detection, you can make data-driven decisions to enhance crop health, optimise yields, and ensure food safety. Our platform also facilitates traceability, enabling you to meet the increasing demands of global markets.
